Our golf course

Just a 15 minute walk from the UoL campus lies the Liverpool Executive Golf Course, an 18 hole oasis in the midst of this metropolitan city. It offers unspoiled views over greenery and is suitable for all golf enthusiasts, beginners and professionals alike. The challenging, yet golfer friendly parkland course, features mature trees, several ponds and strategic bunkering. Kept in excellent condition all year round, all visitors are welcome to enjoy an exiting game of golf and relaxing at our bar and restaurant.


Contact us if you are interested in our 'Introduction to golf' package, which includes a one-hour lesson with a certified trainer and three hour course access.
