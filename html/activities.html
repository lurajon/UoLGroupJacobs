Activities

Liverpool and the surrounding area is brimming with things to see and do. Liverpool city is renowned for its architectural sights alongside a diverse collection of museums, such as the Beatles museum, and attractions. Its compact city centre offers irresistible shopping, plenty of restaurants and bars. Other unique experiences include the possibility to attend premier league football matches and a vibrant nightlife.

The modern UoL Hotel offers a wide array of services and amenities, ranging from spacious guest rooms to delicious on-site dining options. In addition to our traditional hotel and restaurant services cited, we offer our guests and other visitors a unique spa experience and access to an 18 hole golf court just a stone throw from campus.
