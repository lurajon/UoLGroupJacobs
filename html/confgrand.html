Spacious Single Bed and Double Bed Guest Rooms

Conference Room "Grand"

Choosing our conference room provides for peace of mind, as our staff will take care of arranging all your venue, catering and technical needs. UoL Hotel delivers consistency and reliability.

 

Conference Room "Grand" is 29 feet wide by 100 feet long. Seating capacity for the whole undivided room is 100 people, with approximately 50 when divided, depending on the configuration of the tables and chairs. The pull-across divider fits into slots on the West wall and then each panel hooks into the next one. When not pulled across, it rests in the recess in the East wall. Please take care when pulling these panels and fitting them into the slots in order to prevent damage. We expect you to courteously cooperate with your fellow users when there are 2 groups using the rooms during the same time period. (Oregon)

 

The North side (the one closest to the Extension Office) has a full kitchen with a refrigerator, stove, microwave and sink. The South side (closest to the end of the building) has a sink, microwave and two fountains. Both rooms have access from the level parking lot with a curb cut for wheel chairs by the main building entrance. There are accessible restrooms and a courtesy phone for local calls located in the Conference Room lobby. (Oregon)

 

During breakout sessions your attendees can dig into the healthy refreshments whilst relaxing in large, comfortable and dedicated spaces, engage with each other, read the daily newspapers or check their emails on the free Internet kiosks and complimentary wireless Internet access. (Cliftons)

Amenities

  • Wi-Fi & High-speed DSL Internet access (free)
  • Data projector
  • Flip-chart
  • Whiteboard
  • Laptop or PC for instructor
  • 8 cabled microphones at the speaker tables
  • 10 roving microphones in the room for questions from the audience (if simultaneous translation is being used, cabled microphones and stands will be provided to minimize interference)
  • 2 full-time technician located at the back of the room
  • 5 full-time member of staff to check badges and generally oversee proceedings (WorldHearFed)
