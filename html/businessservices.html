Business Services

Staying at a UoL hotel should never limit your productivity during your business trips. Here the UoL Hotel, we provide the business traveller and students with a variety of services to meet their business and educational requirements.

 

Services include:

 

  • Meeting and conference rooms
  • Audio and video conferencing
  • Free wireless internet access
  • Printing and photocopy services
  • Fax services that include free outgoing fax
  • complimentary newspaper
  • complimentary parking available
