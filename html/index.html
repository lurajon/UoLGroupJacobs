Welcome to your home away from Home!

UoL Hotel is a University of Liverpool owned and managed off-campus hotel built with primary intent for providing boarding, temporary residence, for visiting alumni and students.

 

Today it provides full range of services from simple bed and breakfast to organisation of small-scale conferences and events for its guests regardless to their affiliation to the UOL.

 

Affordable prices and services offered make it a quite popular hotel choice amongst Liverpool visitors as well as UOL alumni and students.

 

Hotel provides for 4 Single Bed rooms and 4 Double Bed rooms as well as 2 Conference rooms: Conference Grand accepting up to 100 participants and Conference Medium accepting up to 40 participants.

 

The modern UoL Hotel offers wide array of services and amenities, ranging from spacious guestrooms to delicious on-site dining options. The art of blending modern luxury and impeccable service with a premier setting has been perfected at the incredible hotel set.
