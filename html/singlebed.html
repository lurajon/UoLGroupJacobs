Spacious Single Bed Rooms

Newly renovated guestrooms feature a contemporary color scheme with contrasting warm and cool gray and orange hues along with numerous improvements and environmental enhancements.

 

Stay connected at an oversized work desk with an ergonomic swivel chair, Wi-Fi and high speed Internet access and data port. Relax in the comfortable lounge chair in the spacious sitting area, as you entertain yourself with an iHome alarm clock, cable movie channels or video on demand on the 37” plasma TV.

 

Rest in sublime comfort on one King Size Bed, fitted with fine linens, down blanket and plump pillows. In the morning, rise and shine with an invigorating shower in the deluxe bathroom with luxurious Portico bath amenities. All our sophisticated downtown Chicago hotel rooms feature convenient video account review, messaging and check-out. (Hyatt)
