Spa

The UoL Hotel's onsite spa offers visitors a complete and relaxing spa experience. In our day spa, you can bathe in the warm, mineral-rich waters and choose from a range of spa treatments designed to ease the body and soothe the mind. UoL Hotel spa takes pleasure in offering 3 exclusive treatment rooms, in addition to an indoors swimming pool, steam room, sauna and changing rooms. The spa offers a selection of spa-related treatments including massage, facials and body treatments.


UoL Hotel's guests are eligible for our special "Spa package" which includes an overnight stay in one of our guest rooms, a two hour long spa experience and a dinner in our restaurant.


We welcome you to the UoL Hotel Spa experience!
