Spacious Single Bed and Double Bed Guest Rooms

Conference Room "Medium"

Choosing our conference room provides for peace of mind, as our staff will take care of arranging all your venue, catering and technical needs. UoL Hotel delivers consistency and reliability.

 

Conference Room "Medium" has 621 square feet and can accommodate 20-40 people depending on room configuration. The top table is set up to allow for several speakers in a row, and this can be extended/adjusted according to your needs. Our Event Advisors are here to help you with everything. From the things you can’t see, such as individually climate controlled room areas and commercial grade lighting through to the things you can’t help but notice such as generous breakout areas and continuous complimentary tea, coffee and biscuits – UoL Hotel ensures your event is a success.

 

During breakout sessions your attendees can dig into the healthy refreshments whilst relaxing in large, comfortable and dedicated spaces, engage with each other, read the daily newspapers or check their emails on the free Internet kiosks and complimentary wireless Internet access. (Cliftons)

Amenities

  • Wi-Fi & High-speed DSL Internet access (free)
  • Data projector
  • Flip-chart
  • Whiteboard
  • Laptop or PC for instructor
  • 4 cabled microphones at the speaker table
  • 2 roving microphones in the room for questions from the audience (if simultaneous translation is being used, cabled microphones and stands will be provided to minimize interference)
  • 1 full-time technician located at the back of the room
  • 1 full-time member of staff to check badges and generally oversee proceedings
