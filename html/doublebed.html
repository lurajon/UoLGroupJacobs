Luxurious Double Bed Rooms

Settle into your favorite side of the room (since there are two Queen Grand Beds™) and enjoy a full range of amenities in these spacious downtown Liverpool hotel rooms. Newly renovated guestrooms feature a contemporary color scheme with contrasting warm and cool gray and orange hues along with numerous improvements and environmental enhancements.

 

Stay connected at an oversized work desk with an ergonomic swivel chair, Wi-Fi and high speed Internet access and data port. Relax in the comfortable lounge chair in the spacious sitting area, as you entertain yourself with an iHome alarm clock, cable movie channels or video on demand on the 37” plasma TV.

 

Sink into the two Double Grand Beds™, fitted with fine linens, a luxuriously soft down blanket and plump pillows, or relax in a comfortable armchair in the spacious sitting area. A wall-mounted hairdryer, iron and ironing board, and makeup mirror in our Deluxe downtown Liverpool hotel rooms help you look your best. (Hyatt)
